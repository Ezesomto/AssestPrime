About us • Asset Alchemy Prime

INVESTING IN BUSINESS INFRASTRUCTURE

We are an investment company providing first-rate infrastructure for businesses around the world. Most of our clients are global brands in the e-commerce, processing industry and logistics sectors. We own a network of 54 industrial technologies in the United Kingdom, Poland, Germany, Slovakia, the Netherlands, and Spain with legal certification that guarantees a sustainable and friendly approach to the financial market. We focus on various aspect of finance and wealth management by providing access to over 4,000 funds and assets listed on the major capital markets.

United Nations Global Compact

Since 2023, we have been participants of the United Nations Global Compact initiative, which brings together companies striving to push the boundaries in the areas of human rights, employment, the environment and the fight against corruption.

Criminal & Ethical Compliance Program

We are fully aware of our responsibility towards our business partners, investors and employees. We treat everybody in a professional and open manner, always following our principles. The Aphex Group implemented a special Criminal Compliance Program. It includes the Criminal Compliance Code and the Code of Ethics. These documents present the measures to prevent crime and unethical practices – such measures apply to all individuals working for Aphex.

If you suspect that a crime was committed or unethical practices were applied, do not hesitate to contact support@aphexprime.org or help@aphexprime.org.
